CONTROLS

- Keys arrows for movement
- ESC for exit
- ENTER to restart the game

ABOUT THE GAME

This game's goal is simple; you have to try
pushing the max amount of balls possible into
the cylinder in the middle before the time runs out.
You'll see your final score on the tittle of the .exe,
also, the balls will turn orange and red when the time is running
out. Good luck!
